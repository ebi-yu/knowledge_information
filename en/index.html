"Knowledge" is an open-source knowledge base platform.

About Knowledge
Free
Service is free. This is open source.
Easy Setup
Easy Setup. Only download the file and put it in your server.
Mobile
It is corresponding to the display of the PC and mobile and tablet.
Markdown
You will edit lightly in Markdown. the registered content, you can specify whether to publish to anyone.
Find
You can find knowledge with full-text search. also you can also grouping by the tag.
Notification
Information of registration, to inform you of events such as the evaluation and comments in the "e-mail" or "desktop notifications".
Attach File
It will treat the attachment. Since also extract content that is written in the file, you will be able to search in the contents.
Social
Comments of the exchange, it will activate the communication in such registration of evaluation.


Knowledge Mnagement

Let's proceed with knowledge management in the "knowledge". It supports PC / tablet / smartphone. Anytime, anywhere you can share information.


Self-hosted

Knowledge is installed on your server, the data is stored in a database that you own.
You do not need to worry, that your important data is leak.

Integration of user management

You can use the account of ActiveDirectory and Ldap. If there is no AD or LDAP, you will user management in the system.


You can reach information

You find the information they want to use a full-text search engine.
Search target is not only registered text on web page.
You can search for a text that was written in the attached Excel or Word.

Registration details will be notified to you when the article is registered. Notification method, "e-mail notification," "desktop notifications", "WebHook" are available.


Select the publication range

You can specify the publication range of the registered information. You can publish the information to all users. On the contrary, it will be able to make visible your information only to yourself.

"Knowledge" is the ability of the group management. You are able to publish the information by specifying the group.


Activation

You can be edited easily with Markdown. The information you want to register, you can add an attachment file.

Your registered information will be registered "Comments" and "Like". A feature of social communication, information sharing is activated.
